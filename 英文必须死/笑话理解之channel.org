* English Channel
#+begin_example
  Teacher: "Where is the English Channel?"

  Pupil: "I don't know, my TV doesn't pick it up!"
#+end_example

=Channel= 有海峡的意思， =English Channel= 是指英吉利海峡。

同时 =Channel= 还有 =电视频道= 的意思，所以  =English Channel= 还可以表示英语频道。所以学生才会说电视上找不到
