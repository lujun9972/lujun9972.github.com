* How are men like noodles?
#+begin_example
  How are men like noodles?

  They are always in hot water, they lack taste, and they need dough.
#+end_example

这个双关比较多：
+ =hot water= 即是 =热水= 也表示 =困境=
+ =taste= 即是 =味道= 也表示 =品位=
+ =dough= 即是 =生面团= 在美国俚语中也表示 =金钱=
